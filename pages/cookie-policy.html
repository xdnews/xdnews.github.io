The website mmfoot.com uses cookies to ensure we provide the best experience for our visitors. By accessing and using this website, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. Below is an explanation of what cookies are, the types of cookies we use, and how you can manage cookies on your device.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or activities on the website.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use various types of cookies to enhance the functionality of the website and ensure an optimal experience. Below are the types of cookies used on mmfoot.com:

2.1 Functional Cookies

These cookies are necessary to ensure our website functions properly. For example, these cookies enable you to navigate pages smoothly and use essential features like forms or login options.

2.2 Analytical Cookies

Analytical cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. This information allows us to improve website performance, such as ensuring pages load quickly or identifying the most frequently visited pages.

2.3 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to display ads relevant to your interests. We work with third parties to place these cookies. The information collected may include pages you visit, products you view, or links you click.

3. Third-Party Use of Cookies

We also use third-party services such as Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These third parties may place cookies on your device to collect specific information, such as your IP address, browser type, or pages you visit. This information is used for analytical and marketing purposes.

4. How to Manage Cookies?

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish through your browser settings. Below are some options you can consider:

  • Delete cookies: You can delete all cookies already stored on your device through your browser settings.
  • Block cookies: You can configure your browser to block all cookies or only specific cookies. Please note that if you block cookies, some features on our website may not work correctly.

For more information on how to manage cookies, you can visit www.aboutcookies.org.

5. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to check this page periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
